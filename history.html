Learn About Notable Figures in Junk Journaling

Teesha Moore

Teesha's work contains a lot of mixed media; she always uses bright colours as she describes that it makes her 'feels more alive'. In her media, she incorporates computer fonts, newspaper, painting, drawing, and many more. Her work has been shown globally and has had a lot of press on it.
