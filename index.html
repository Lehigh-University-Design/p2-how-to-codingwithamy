About

Welcome to my corner of creativity! This website is dedicated to inspiring others to dive into the world of junk journaling. Whether you’re a seasoned artist or just starting out, I believe everyone can find joy in repurposing materials and expressing their unique stories. Join me in turning junk into amazing artwork.


Benefits

Junk journey is a great way to explore one’s creative side. Whether you believe you are an artist or not, I personally believe anyone can succeed at junk journaling. This activity is an excellent way to take time to oneself and grow as an individual. Apart from boosting creativity, it also reduces stress, promotes mindfulness, and great for personal reflection. Additionally, it serves as a memory book to document important moments through physical and visual means. By keeping scraps and repurposing them in journals, you are also supporting the environment by being sustainable!
