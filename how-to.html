How To Essemble The Journal?

Step 1: Choose a page in your journal

Step 2: Pick Your Scraps

Take out your saved up junk scraps and any materials you want to use.

Step 3: Cutting

Start cutting your images and scraps.

Step 4:Positioning

Now position where you want your elements to be placed.

Step 5: Glue it Down!

Glue down your elements.

Step 6: Details (optional)

Draw in any details with a pen.

Step 7: Journal Entry

Write about your day! What happened today? Anything memorable you want your future self to remember?

Step 8: Congradulations!

Now you’re done!
